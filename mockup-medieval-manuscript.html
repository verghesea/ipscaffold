Figura I: The Problem of Many Cables

Imagine, good Reader, having a desk cluttered with charging cables for thy telephone, portable computing device, time-keeping apparatus, and ear instruments for the reception of music. Each device requireth its own cable, and thou art constantly searching for the proper charger. This is not merely vexing—'tis a limitation of our current arts and sciences.

Traditional methods of charging require physical connexions betwixt a source of power and thy device. This createth several afflictions:

  • Cable wear and deterioration leading to frequent replacements
  • Incompatible standards of charging across diverse devices
  • Limited mobility whilst the device receiveth power
  • Hazards to safety from damaged cables

What if, dear Scholar, thou couldst walk into a chamber and have all thy devices charge automatically, without any cables or manual connexion? This is the promise of the invention herein described.

The Central Question:
How might one transmit the essence of power through the very aether itself,
without need of copper wire or physical touch?

Figura III: The Mechanisms in Detail

The technology operateth upon the principle of magnetic resonance coupling. Herein lies the step-by-step process, as revealed through careful study and experimentation:

Primus — Power Transmission: The base station generateth an alternating current through its coil, thereby creating an oscillating magnetic field at a frequency most specific (typically 6.78 million cycles per second, an industry standard adopted by learned societies).

Secundus — Resonance Coupling: When a device bearing a receiver coil tuned to the selfsame frequency entereth this magnetic field, resonance doth occur—much as how a tuning fork vibrateth when another matching fork be struck in its presence.

Tertius — Power Conversion: The receiver coil converteth the magnetic field back into electrical current, which is then regulated to the appropriate voltage for charging the device's repository of power (commonly called a "battery").

Quartus — Optimization of Efficiency: Intelligent sensors continuously monitor the coupling efficiency and adjust the transmission of power to minimize waste of energy and prevent excessive heating.
